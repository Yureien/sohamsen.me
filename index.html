About me in 50 words

  • If the header wasn't clear enough, my name is Soham Sen.
  • I'm an undergrad at Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur
  • There, I am a member of Kharagpur Open Source Society
  • I like to code stuff, both for fun and profit
  • I also like to play with servers, and deploy and automate stuff (what the cool kids call "devops" nowadays)

I wanna know more!

  • I like to wear a black hoodie, get a green terminal and bash the keyboard keys. That is, I like to pretend I am a hacker. More accurately, I participate in cybersecurity competitions (CTFs).
  • I love receiving and transmitting radio waves. Like listening to weather satellites transmitting a picture. Or creating my own LTE basestation.
  • Sometimes I tinker with electronics. And build a robot or two. Or automate something IRL.
  • I love space. Not the blank but the cosmos. My childhood dream was/is to become an astronaut. I like to participate in astronomy competitions and gaze at the universe with a telescope.
  • I watch anime, and have seen quite a lot. Also read mangas, and both English and Japanese novels.
  • Check out my blog. There, I sometimes document the stuff I am doing.

Projects worth mentioning

I don't really get the time to work on new projects nowadays, but here are some I made in the past:

  • Pinocchio: A Discord bot (in 5000+ servers, last I checked) aimed at anime lovers
  • YTSwag: A chrome extension designed to make Youtube Music better and more usable. Adds lyrics, PIP mode, themes and more
  • SPlaying: A simple worker on Cloudflare that fetches songs a user is playing, if any. Being used in this site!
  • nginx-mime-magic-module: An nginx module to automatically set the MIME type of a file from its contents
This website was made without CSS frameworks, without gigabytes of content and a tiny bit of optional JS.
